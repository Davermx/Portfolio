About Me

A litle history

I will begin my story when my perception of technology changed, it was 1994 with just 18 years old ("when you think nothing can surprise you"), now I laugh inside myself. At that time I studied high school in the morning and in the afternoon I would play basketball all afternoon. My mother tired of my waste of time and laziness. She tells me from one day to the next, Son, I just enrolled you in a computer school, this news was taken as an insult, "as it is possible that she did it without asking me", reluctantly I told her that several acquaintances. Calmly she only gave an instruction, and it was tomorrow you show up to the classes of 16:30. and I said to myself, well, I'll introduce myself tomorrow but I don't think it will be more than a month, and the next day I still bothered to force myself to attend classes that I didn't want, as I was going to stop going to the game of stars of all afternoons with my friends and my girlfriend at the Benito Juarez Auditorium.

My world changed in 45 min.

It all started with, a good afternoon young people, my name is Fernando Becerril and I will be his teacher in the career of analyst programmer, he began to give his class and explain what we would be able to do if we finished the degree. continue explaining his class and without realizing it I was caught by all the anecdotes he shared and 45 minutes later the class was over. I could not believe I had finished the class, and I was just thinking, but if it was a short time I want to know more ... so I had to wait another day to continue with what we had stayed, still leaving the building with so much energy and enthusiasm that never I had experimented, I was thinking THIS IS MINE and as we say the jarochos "I'm going to put all the kilos on him "in every class and every task they put in. On laboratory days it was like being at NASA a room full of computers and one was, for me, THE PARADISE ...
